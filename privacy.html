Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how our screen recording website handles your personal information as well as the recorded content generated by using our software.

What Information Do We Collect?

Our website does not collect any user data or personal information. We do not require you to provide any personal information to access or use our website. Further, we do not use cookies or any other tracking technology to monitor your activity.

Do We Store Your Recorded Content?

We do not store any recorded content generated by our software. The recorded content is saved directly onto your device or computer. Once you have finished recording, you can choose to upload the content to a cloud storage provider or any other service of your choice. It is your responsibility to ensure that your recordings are stored and used in accordance with the applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Third-Party Websites

This privacy policy does not apply to any third-party websites or services that you choose to access through our website. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third-party websites or services.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time, at our sole discretion. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to understand how we are protecting your personal information.

Contact Information
